### Overview

Rosters of delegates to the 1787 Constitutional Convention and state ratifying conventions.

### Source

Historical records of convention attendance. Contact: chrisliao (at) uchicago (dot) edu.

### When/where obtained & original form of files

Spreadsheets obtained via SPEOC dropbox and converted from xlsx into csv. Contact: chrisliao (at) uchicago (dot) edu.


### Description

**`orig/`** 
- `constitutional_convention_1787.csv` — Names of delegates to the 1787 Constitutional Convention in Philadelphia.
- `state_delegates.csv` — Names of delegates to each state's ratifying convention.

### Terms of Use

Data is derived from public historical records.
